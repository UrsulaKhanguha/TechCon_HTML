History

The first TechCon North America conference was held in New Orleans, Louisiana in 1996. The conference has since moved around the United States to accommodate the growing number of attendees. The conference features industry experts leading discussions on technical development and solutions.

Mission

TechCon conferences aim to bring together industry leaders, innovators, and professionals to discuss and showcase advancements in technology and innovation. For instance, TechCon SoCal 2025 focuses on cutting-edge technologies such as AI, digital health, robotics, consumer tech, data, software, and semiconductors, fostering exponential growth in these areas. BIOCOM. Similarly, the SVC TechCon 2025's mission is to promote technical excellence by providing a global forum to inform, educate, and engage members, the technical community, and the public on all aspects of vacuum coating. EVENTS IN AMERICA.Each TechCon event may have a specific focus, but the overarching mission is to drive collaborative innovation and progress in the technology sector.
